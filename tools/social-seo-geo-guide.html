What is Social SEO?

Search Engine Optimization on social media means using specific keywords, alt text, and account structures so that when users search for a topic (e.g., "coding tips"), your profile or post appears first in the app's search bar.

What is GEO? (The Future)

Generative Engine Optimization is optimizing your brand so that AI engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) recommend YOU as the authoritative answer. It relies on brand mentions, high-value structured data, and context rather than just keywords.

Platform-Specific SEO Playbooks

Instagram SEO

  • Name Field Optimization: Don't just put your name. Put "Name | Niche" (e.g., Mihir | Web Developer). The Name field is highly searchable.
  • On-Screen Text (Reels): Insta's AI reads the text you type on your video. Use primary keywords in the first 3 seconds of text.
  • Alt Text Customization: Go to 'Advanced Settings' before posting an image and manually write descriptive Alt Text loaded with keywords.

YouTube SEO & GEO

  • Filename Matters: Rename your raw .mp4 and .jpg (thumbnail) files to your target keyword BEFORE uploading.
  • Timestamps (Chapters): AI engines love structured data. Adding chapters (e.g., 01:20 What is HTML?) allows Google Search and AI to index specific parts of your video.
  • First 200 Words: YouTube's speech recognition indexes what you say. Say your main keywords out loud in the first 30 seconds.

LinkedIn Growth SEO

  • Headline Formula: [Role] | [Keywords you want to be found for] | [Value you provide]. Keep it under 220 characters.
  • Creator Mode & Hashtags: Turn on Creator mode and select 5 core hashtags. Sprinkle these exact tags naturally in your 'About' section.
  • Dwell Time: The algorithm pushes posts that keep users reading. Use formatting, bullet points, and "hook" opening lines to increase read time.

Twitter / X Algorithm

  • Thread formatting: X favors long-form reading. Start a thread with a powerful hook and drop value in consecutive posts to boost overall impression stacking.
  • Engagement Velocity: Replies within the first 15 minutes of posting weigh heavily. Engage with others right before and after you post.
  • Visual Assets: Tweets with images, GIFs, or native video get a significant algorithm boost compared to plain text or external links.

How to Rank in AI (GEO Strategy)

Generative Engines don't just look for keywords; they look for **Authority, Factuality, and Citations**. Here is how to make ChatGPT and Gemini recommend you and your brand.

1. Co-Citation

Mention highly authoritative brands in your niche next to your own brand name. AI learns associations. If you talk about "Web Development", mention top frameworks.

2. Structure is King

AI loves formatted data. Use Bullet points, Numbered lists, and clear H2/H3 headings. AI bots scrape structured answers much faster than huge blocks of text.

3. Unique Perspectives

AI already knows the generic answers. To be cited by an AI, your content must offer unique data, personal case studies, or strong expert opinions.
