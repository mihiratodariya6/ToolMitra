How to Convert Words to Numbers

Converting large financial figures or statistical data from text to numerical digits can be prone to human error. ToolMitra's AI-assisted parsing engine reads English numerical text and converts it into accurate digits instantly, saving time for accountants, bankers, and students.

International System

Groups numbers by thousands. Used globally.

  • 1 Thousand = 1,000
  • 1 Million = 1,000,000
  • 1 Billion = 1,000,000,000

Indian System

Groups numbers by hundreds (after 1,000). Used in South Asia.

  • 1 Lakh = 1,00,000
  • 1 Crore = 1,00,000,000
  • 1 Arab = 1,00,00,00,000
