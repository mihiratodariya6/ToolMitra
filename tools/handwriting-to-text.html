How does the Global Image to Text (OCR) Tool Work?

ToolMitra uses advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology to help users worldwide. When you upload an image, our client-side AI analyzes the pixels and converts them into editable digital text. Because the processing happens entirely in your browser, your sensitive documents never leave your device, ensuring 100% privacy.
